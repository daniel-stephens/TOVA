Step-by-step instructions

How these steps connect—from upload, through your corpus and training, to dashboards and results.

From raw text to topics

A guided path used in research and analytics: ingest documents, configure preprocessing, pick a model family, then watch training progress with an optional low-latency preview.

  • Corpus library keeps versions organized and reusable across training runs.
  • Job-aware UI resumes the right screen with model and corpus context.
  • Trained models catalog opens dashboards in one click.

Classical & LLM workflows

Choose traditional probabilistic models for speed and reproducibility, or LLM-backed pipelines when you need richer labels and narrative structure.

  • Classical topic models for scalable inference on large corpora.
  • LLM configurations for summarization and labeling—with keys managed server-side.
  • YAML-backed global config so defaults stay consistent for your team.

Dashboards you can explore

After training, open a model dashboard for theme overview charts, per-document assignments, search and filters, and optional assistant chat grounded in your project.

  • Theme distributions and diagnostics at a glance.
  • Document table with themes, text drill-down, and exports.
  • Run inference on new text without leaving the workspace.

Ready when you are

Sign in to sync preferences, manage corpora and models across sessions, and (for administrators) oversee users and resources from a single console.

  • Accounts with per-user configuration overlays.
  • Upload and validate files before they enter the pipeline.
  • Use the account menu for global configuration used during training and labeling.
